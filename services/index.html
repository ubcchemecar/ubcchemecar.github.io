Affordability

At $0.20/gram, we offer the CHEAPEST service on campus. Send your .STL files and pick up your parts in the CHBE buiding.

Flexibility

Need something printed over the weekend or 3am at night? We can help. *Additional charges may apply if you REALLY need us to print it at 3am.

Turnaround Time

1 week or less depending on how busy the printer is, but if this is an emergency let us know and we will try our best to accomodate.

Speed

Turnaround time also depends on several factors including how big your part(s) are and the print resolution, e.g. LOW - 3mm, MED - 1mm, HIGH - 0.3mm

Printer

We are using the LulzBot Taz 6. Printer specifications are available here

Need something printed? Send us your .STL files.

Please use mm for your dimensions. Pick up in the CHBE building. Cash on delivery. We issue invoices.

If you need to fit multiple parts together, please use at least 0.3mm tolerance in your model for best results.

Small parts are tricky to print, we recommend a minimum of 1mm or 2mm wall thickness. If you have REALLY small parts, please contact us first to discuss your model.
