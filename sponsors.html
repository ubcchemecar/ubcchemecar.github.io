Funding and Sponsorship

We appreciate the kind support and funding provided by following organizations:

UBC Department of Chemical and Biological Engineering

The Department provides workshop services and lab space for the team to conduct experiments safely and has been very supportive of all Chem-E-Car activities.

Precision Nanosystems

Precision Nanosystems is a frontrunner in the development of nanomedicines and genetic modelling. Their technology allows for research to be performed 20X faster than the gold standard systems while also being 10X less expensive.

Solidworks

Solidworks has kindly provided UBC Chem-E-Car with Solidworks software for all our designing needs.

The University of British Columbia

Chem-E-Car is funded by The Faculty of Applied Science Professional Activities Fund (PAF) and the Shell Engineering Fund

DigitalOcean

As described in their website, DigitalOcean is a cloud infrastructure provider focused on simplifying web infrastructure for software developers. They have kindly provided support and funding for our hosting and software development needs.

We Value Your Support

UBC Chem-E-Car provides an opportunity for undergraduate students to gain relevant engineering and project management experience. Our past successes are the result of every member contributing their unique skill sets to the project and working together as a team.

Building the best Chem-E-Car requires the enthusiasm and persistence of our team members. It also requires, among others, sophisticated electrochemistry equipment, lab-grade chemicals and many electronic and mechanical components. The support of the UBC community and our sponsors is essential for propelling the team to greater heights.

We want everyone on the team to reach their full potential. To do so, we need your help.

Our sponsors are recognized for their contributions in many ways. Aside from displaying your company logo in our events and providing advertorial space on our blog and website, we create networking opportunities for you to engage with some of the best students in UBC. Becoming a sponsor allows you to mentor and foster meaningful relationships with UBC's most diligent students and scout out future assets for your organization.

If you are interested in sponsoring UBC Chem-E-Car, please download our sponsorship package below.

How We Represent You

Student Outreach

Connect with the UBC's most diligent and enthusiastic students. UBC has a community of over 50,000 students and faculty. Your logo will be included in the Department of Chemical Engineering newsletter, on our website and Facebook page, and events posters.

AiChE National Conference

Your logo will also be placed on our car and our competition t-shirts which will be seen at the AIChE National Conference held anually in the US. More than 30 university teams as well as industry professionals across North America will be there.

Where Your Contributions Go

Construction and Machining

We are one of the few student teams in UBC that do not have a dedicated student machine shop and must outsource machining work.

Chemicals and Equipment

We need lab-grade chemicals, lab equipment (beakers, pipettes, rubber gloves and etc.) and electronic components (Arduino, multimeters, lasers etc.)

Travel Expenses

Ideally, we would like our entire team to experience and show off their hard work at the competition but we are constrained by travel expenses.
